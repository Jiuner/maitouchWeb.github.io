Quick and stable triggering, creating a safe environment of zero-touch!

  • 01 Touchless Elevator Ride

    m'AI Touch can allow users to incorporate this epidemic prevention technology seamlessly into their daily lives while maintaining their original operating habits, so that everyone can enjoy the "zero-touch" elevator ride experience.

  • 02 Effectively Reduce Resource Waste

    Compared to the need to spend a lot of manpower to regularly clean antimicrobial films and alcohol disinfection, m'AI Touch can prevent virus transmission more efficiently and reduce the generation of resource waste.

  • 03 Effectively Promote Hygiene and Health

    The probability of the presence of the coronavirus on the elevator buttons after contact by COVID-19 patients reaches 43% (Wiktorczyk-Kapischke et al.,2021; Wu et al., 2019). Therefore, the elevator riding experience created by m'AI Touch reduces the risk of virus contact and can effectively promote social hygiene and health.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Which elevators can be installed with m'AI Touch? Are there any restrictions on brands?

    m'AI Touch can be installed on any brand of elevator, old and new, because it only requires a lunchbox-sized device to be fitted above the elevator panel, without any modifications to the existing panel. It is as safe and convenient as installing an access control system, so there are no restrictions on the elevator brand and model.

  • Do I need to remove gloves when using it?

    If you work in hospitals or laboratories, you don't need to worry about the inconvenience of having to remove your medical gloves. m'AI Touch allows you to operate the elevator smoothly without taking off your gloves, relieving you of the burden of putting on and taking off gloves and using alcohol for disinfection.

  • What are the advantages compared to other brands?

    m'AI Touch has high installation convenience—it is suitable for any brand of elevator, old and new, and is also applicable in any cultural context or scenario. It has a fast and stable trigger rate and can retain original usage habits, making it effectively applicable in various settings.
